Creating your own Chimera

Within this app you can create your own chimera, or let the whims of fate do their thing and let one be randomly generated!

A Chimera needs a name, a Head and a Torso, but Wings, Legs and a Tail are optional. Clear the Selection boxes to see your options or let the random button choose for you

Once created click on the Chimeras Animal parts to take you to their wikipedia page!
